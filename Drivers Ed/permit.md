# Permit   
- Permit   
    - Make an appoint with the RMV or AAA if have a membership    
    - 14 page document from the RMV on the website on how to get a permit   
    - Have to prove lawful presence 
        - prove that you life in Massachusetts   
    - Cannot make an appointment until you are 16   
    - Go to the registry's website and fill out the permit application ahead of time.    
        - Can't fill it out until you are 16   
    - Have to go with a parent   
    - Standard License v Real ID   
        - Real ID is a federal program to eliminate illegal immigrants   
        - Mandated Massachusetts    
        - Both need a I.D need 2 out of the three   
            1. Passport that is not expired    
            2. Social security card that is not laminated    
            3. Original Birth Certificate with a raised red seal   
        - Proof of address has to be kids name, could be a school transcript or bank statement but has to be within 60 days. 2 proofs of transcripts for real ID one proof standard ID   
        - Without Real ID can not get into any federal buildings without a Real ID. Can get into federal buildings with a Standard ID but need another piece of ID with you.    
    - $30 for the permit, $50 for the license, 35 for the road test. Pay for all of them at once   
    - Costs $25 to upgrade from a standard to a real   
    - Before permit test   
        - Eye Exam can have up to 20/40 until you need contacts central and peripheral    
            - If you need contacts will be on permit as corrective lens   
            - If you do not have corrective lens on the day of the test it will be a rejection    
            - can only take a road test 6 times on a permit. Rejections do not count toward the 6.    
        - Picture   
        - SA number   
            - is like you ss number. Record of driving mistakes forever   
            - SA number means you are a real id holder   
            - Parents have S number   
        - Sign your name    
            - Giving Implied Consent    
            - Melanie's law   
        - Give you a sheet of a paper with a code can either take the permit test at the RMV or within 6 months at home. If taking it at home cameras are mandatory    
        - Can get help with studying with a an app called DMV genie, cs also has some permit tests   
        - Test   
            - 25 questions   
            - need to get 18   
            - can skip 7   
            - all questions are multiple choice.    
            - Most of the questions are about fines and signs    
        - License Fee is $50 every 5 years   
        - MA, CA, RI, NH, VT, NY, with a Massachusetts permit   
        - **After getting a permit go to the portal and enter the permit #, issue date and the permit date, and expiration date. **   
        - Road test   
            - Two options    
                - Go with parents   
                    - Have to use parents car,  can not use a vehicle with an emergency brake in the center console that can be pulled or pushed so the examiner can stop the car in the case of an emergency    
                    - Costs less    
                    - Have to have all six requirements done before tests   
                    - Have to book it with a RMV and could be far away    
                    - Need a road test application form bring it with you and parents have to sign it   
                    - Need a sponsor could be parent or someone 21 plus with a valid license for 1 year    
                        - Protection   
                        - Witness
